### What You CAN Do (No Permission Needed)

- Use the source code to build adapters for personal use

- Create derivative works under Apache 2.0 terms
- Sell hardware running USBRetro firmware (with your own branding)
- Reference USBRetro in documentation (e.g., "Compatible with USBRetro firmware")

### What REQUIRES Permission

- Using "USBRetro" or product names in your commercial product name or branding
- Implying official endorsement, partnership, or sponsorship

This policy allows the community to freely use and build upon USBRetro while ensuring that commercial use of the brand supports continued development. Revenue from commercial licensing helps fund hardware, testing, and ongoing maintenance.
